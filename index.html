Simple, Private, Local Banking for Your Small Projects

A lightweight personal banking demo that keeps your data in your browser. Fast, private, and easy to use — perfect for learning, prototyping, or managing simple personal ledgers.

Get Started — Create an Account

Local-first & Private

All account data is stored in your browser's localStorage — nothing leaves your device unless you export it. Great for privacy and quick demos.

Instant Updates

Create accounts, deposit, withdraw, and rename instantly. Changes are saved automatically so your data persists on refresh.

Minimal & Easy

A small, focused interface so anyone can track balances without complexity. Ideal for students and small personal projects.

Why this banking system?

It’s designed to be simple, transparent, and portable. Since it uses localStorage, you can use it offline, keep your data private, and inspect or export your data anytime. It's not a production bank — it's a clean, educational tool you can run and modify in minutes.
